Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1 or 2
Ingredients Quantity
Persian/Japanese cucumbers 1
Avocados 2
Lemon 1/2
Sheets of nori(seaweed) 2
Sushi Rice 2 cups
Shrimp Tempura 8
Tobiko(flying fish roe) 2 Tbsp
Unagi(eel) 1

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.

Step 2: Put shrimp, cucumbers and avocados inside the seaweed and rice and roll.

Step 3: Top with avocado, tobiko and spicy mayo.

Step 4: Enjoy!!

Source: Dragon Roll

Recipe #2

  • Time: 1 hour 42 min
  • Serves: 8-10
    • Ingredients Quantity
    Graham cracker crumbs 1 1/2 cups
    Confectioner's sugar 1/4 cup
    Melted butter 1/3 cup
    Salt 1/2 tsp
    Cream Cheese 2 cups
    Large eggs 2
    Granulated sugar 2/3 cup
    Vanilla extract 1 tsp

    Step 1: Get pie pan

    Step 2: Stir crust ingredients together

    Step 3: Press crumbs on bottom and sides of pan

    Step 4: Mix filling ingredients together until smooth

    Step 5: Set pie pan onto baking sheet and pour filling into the crust

    Step 6: Bake cheesecake in oven for 20 min at 165°F to 170°F

    Step 7: Once done, refrigerate until cool

    Step 8: Enjoy!!

    Source: Easy Cheesecake

    Recipe #3

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 4-5
    • Ingredients Quantity
    All-purpose flour 1 cup
    White sugar 1 tbsp
    Baking powder 2 tsp
    Salt 1/4 tsp
    Beaten egg 1
    Milk 1 cup
    Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
    Mashed ripe bananas 2

    Step 1: Mix dry ingredients and wet ingredients separately

    Step 2: Stir flour mixture into banana mixture

    Step 3: Pour 1/4 scoop onto a heated, lightly oiled pan

    Step 4: Cook until golden brown and enjoy!!

    Source: Banana Pancakes
